# [[PFS-Legal]] Blackfinder's silence

**Source** [_Inner Sea Intrigue pg. 50_](http://paizo.com/products/btpy9l37/discuss&page=5?Pathfinder-Campaign-Setting-Inner-Sea-Intrigue)  
**Price** 400 gp; **Weight** —  
**Type** injury; **Save** Fortitude DC 14  
**Frequency** 1/round for 4 rounds  
**Effect** unable to speak (even to cast spells) for 1 round  
**Cure** 1 save

### Description

This poison is typically used by cultists of Norgorber to silence those who might tell secrets they want kept.
